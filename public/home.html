Welcome To My Pantry

What is "My Pantry?"

Are you a stressed college kid who has to cook for themselves for the first time? Or a mom with kids running around who doesn't have time to search for recipes? Or perhaps just someone who has no clue what to make every night for dinner? Well look no more, "My Pantry" is your solution. "My Pantry" gives you recipe ideas based on the ingredients you have in your household. Want to try a new recipe every day? Visit the Recipe of the Day Page to get a unique recipe each day!
